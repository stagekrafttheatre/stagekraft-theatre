Stagehand

We're seeking reliable, hands-on members for our Stage Setup Crew to support an upcoming community college theatre production! If you're good with physical tasks, quick to adapt, and want a behind-the-scenes experience in live theatre, this could be a great fit.

Responsibilities:

  • Stage Setup: Arrange props, equipment, and set pieces on stage before each rehearsal and performance.
  • Scene Changes: Move props or adjust scenery quickly and quietly during the show.
  • Equipment Handling: Assist with placing light and sound equipment as needed.
  • Post-Show Take Down: Help break down, organize, and store items after each performance or rehearsal.
  • Safety Checks: Ensure all items are secure and the stage is safe and ready for use.

Requirements:

  • Physical Stamina: Able to lift, carry, and move items comfortably.
  • Reliability: Punctuality and dependability are a must.
  • Quick on Your Feet: Able to work quietly and quickly, especially during scene transitions.
  • Attention to Detail: Careful handling of equipment and props.
